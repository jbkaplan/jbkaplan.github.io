PAIRING BOARD

Rails App

Pairing Board is a web application pitched by one of the DBC instructors. Students can set up pairing sessions with each other over the weekend to get help on challenges. Mentors and students are notified when an appointment is made or cancelled, and they can also communicate on the application through a messaging system. Currently, this is accomplished at DBC using a whiteboard, and it is our goal to replace this with a simple, easy-to-use web application.

Technologies:

Rails, Twitter Bootstrap, jQuery, AJAX
